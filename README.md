#3D Golf Simulator

### 3D Unity game: Aim and go for the Ace!
*Ecole 42 Piscine Unity Project*

##### Instructions: 

- Charge power and shoot with Space
- Aim with A and D
- Switch to aerial camera with E
- Switch between clubs with TAB
